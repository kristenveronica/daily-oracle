1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account we collect the email address you sign in with and any display name you provide. Authentication and your account record are managed through our backend provider, Supabase.

Subscription & purchase information

If you start a free trial or subscribe, your purchase is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. We receive your subscription/entitlement status (for example, whether you are on a trial or an active paid plan) and related transaction identifiers. We do not receive or store your full card or bank details — those are handled by Apple, Google, and their payment processors. Website purchases (such as e-books) are processed by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers.

User-generated content

You may voluntarily provide content within the app, including:

  • Ask Odessa messages, and any screenshots or messages you upload for analysis
  • journal entries and written reflections
  • daily check-ins (clarity, self-trust, emotional steadiness)
  • items you save to your Freedom File / Saved Wisdom
  • quiz responses

This content is stored against your account so you can return to it. You are responsible for the content you choose to submit.

Device & technical information

We collect basic technical data needed to run the Services, such as device type, operating system, app version, and a push notification token (see Section 4). On the website we may collect IP address, browser type, referral source, and pages viewed.

Usage information

We collect information about how you interact with the Services — features used and general activity patterns — to operate and improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to provide and improve the Services, specifically to:

  • create and authenticate your account
  • generate Ask Odessa responses and save your conversations
  • deliver the Daily Oracle, the 52-Week Journey, check-ins, and saved content
  • process and manage subscriptions and entitlements
  • send the daily notification at your chosen time
  • provide customer support
  • maintain the safety, security, and integrity of the Services
  • comply with our legal obligations

We may use anonymised or aggregated data (which does not identify you) for analytics and product development. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Ask Odessa & AI Features

Ask Odessa provides educational information, psychoeducation, and reflection. When you send a message — or upload a screenshot or message — that content is sent to our AI provider, Anthropic (the makers of Claude), to generate a response. Odessa's replies are AI-generated.

Your messages are used to generate your response and are not used to train AI models. Anthropic does not use content submitted through its commercial API to train its models. Your conversations are private to your account and are not routinely reviewed by people; a small number of authorised team members may access data only when genuinely needed (for example, to provide support, to keep the Services safe, or where required by law).

AI tools are for education and reflection only and are not therapy, legal advice, medical advice, crisis intervention, diagnosis, or professional risk assessment. Please avoid submitting highly sensitive information where you can.

4. Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, we register a push token through Expo's push service so we can send your daily message. You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings or in the app.

The app includes a Private Notifications setting (on by default) that uses neutral wording on your lock screen so the nature of the app is not revealed. You can change this in Profile → Privacy.

5. Subscriptions & Payments

The Daily Oracle (one daily message) is free. Optional subscriptions unlock the 52-Week Journey and Ask Odessa. Subscriptions are sold and billed by the Apple App Store or Google Play and managed via RevenueCat. Billing, renewals, and refunds are governed by the store's terms and your store account. See our Terms & Conditions for subscription details.

6. Third-Party Services

We share data only with service providers that help us run the Services, under contract and consistent with this Policy:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and secure storage of your account and content
  • Anthropic (Claude) — generates Ask Odessa responses from content you submit (not used to train models)
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription entitlements across platforms
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — process and bill subscriptions
  • Expo — delivers push notifications
  • Stripe — processes website/e-book purchases

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy. We do not currently use third-party advertising networks. If we add an analytics provider, we will update this Policy and this list.

7. Data Storage, Security & International Transfers

Your data is protected with industry-standard measures, including encryption in transit and access controls that restrict your data to your own account. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our providers may store and process data on servers located outside your country (including in the United States). Where personal information is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) as required by applicable law.

8. Data Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. When you delete your account, your associated data is removed from our systems (our providers may retain limited backups for a short period under their own policies). We may retain certain information where required to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

9. Your Rights & Choices

You can access and edit your content in the app, manage notifications in your device or app settings, and manage your subscription in your store account. Depending on where you live, you may also have rights to:

  • access the personal information we hold about you
  • correct inaccurate information
  • request deletion of your information
  • request a copy/portability of your information
  • object to or restrict certain processing, and withdraw consent
  • opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact hello@thedailyoracle.org. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

10. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly in the app: Profile → Account → "Delete my account." This is immediate and cannot be undone. You can also request deletion by email. See our dedicated Account Deletion page for full instructions.
