Getting started

What is The Daily Oracle?

The Daily Oracle is an education and reflection app. It includes a free Daily Oracle message and check-in, plus an optional subscription that unlocks the 52-Week Journey and Ask Odessa. It is not therapy, counselling, medical, legal, or crisis support.

Is the Daily Oracle free?

Yes. The Daily Oracle — one message each day — and the daily Clarity Check-In are free forever. A subscription unlocks the full 52-Week Journey and Ask Odessa.

Subscription questions

What does a subscription include?

The full 52-Week Liberation Journey, Ask Odessa (with weekly credits), saved Journey reflections and progress, and the Freedom File.

Is there a free trial?

New subscribers may receive a 7-day free trial. If you don't cancel before it ends, your subscription begins and renews until cancelled. Current pricing is displayed in the App Store and may vary by country or currency.

How many Ask Odessa questions do I get?

Free users get 1 free Ask Odessa question. Trial users get 7 credits during the trial. Paid subscribers get 7 credits every 7-day subscription period.

Billing questions

How am I billed?

Subscriptions are billed by the Apple App Store or Google Play through your store account. We never see your full card details.

How do I cancel?

iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → The Daily Oracle. Android: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions. Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid the next charge; you keep access until the period ends.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple and Google. For Apple, visit reportaproblem.apple.com; for Google, use the Play Store. See our Terms for details. Your statutory consumer rights still apply.

Account & login

How do I sign in?

You sign in with your email. If you have trouble receiving the sign-in link, check your spam folder or email us.

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Profile → Account → "Delete my account." This permanently removes your account and data. You can also request deletion by email — see our Account Deletion page.

Technical support

The app isn't working / I'm not getting notifications

Make sure you're on the latest version and that notifications are enabled in your device settings. If it persists, email us with your device model and OS version and we'll help.

My subscription isn't showing after purchase

Open the app and use "Restore Purchases" in the subscribe screen or Profile. If it still doesn't appear, email us with the store account email used for the purchase.

Privacy requests

How do I access, correct, or delete my data?

You can edit your content in the app and delete your account at any time. For access, correction, portability, or other privacy requests, email hello@thedailyoracle.org. See our Privacy Policy for your rights.

A note on safety

The Daily Oracle is an education and reflection tool — not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services.
