Delete Your Data

The Daily Oracle · Last updated: June 2026

The Daily Oracle gives you full control over your data. You can delete specific items — without deleting your account — or delete your entire account and all of its data, at any time, directly in the app. Here's exactly how.

Delete specific data (without deleting your account)

Your Ask Odessa conversations

  1. Open the The Daily Oracle app and go to the Ask tab.
  2. Tap the information (ⓘ) icon at the top of the screen.
  3. Tap "Delete my Ask Odessa history."
  4. Confirm. All of your Ask Odessa conversations — including the text and any screenshots you shared in them — are permanently deleted. Nothing else is affected.

A journal entry

  1. Open the app and go to the Saved tab.
  2. Press and hold the journal or incident entry (or open it).
  3. Tap Delete and confirm.

A saved Daily Oracle message

  1. Open the app and go to the Saved tab.
  2. Tap an item to expand it (or press and hold it).
  3. Tap Remove.

A Freedom File entry

  1. Open the app and go to the Saved tab.
  2. Tap the "Freedom Files" filter.
  3. Tap an entry to expand it (or press and hold it), then tap Remove.

Each of these removes that item from your account immediately.

Delete your entire account (all data)

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Tap Account → "Delete my account."
  3. Confirm. Your account and all associated data are permanently removed immediately. This cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address on your account and we'll delete it for you. We action verified requests within 30 days.

Send your deletion request to:

hello@thedailyoracle.org

Request Data Deletion

If the email draft does not open on your device, simply send an email to hello@thedailyoracle.org from the email address associated with your account.

What is deleted, and what is kept

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • your account and profile
  • your check-ins, progress, and clarity tools
  • your journal entries and reflections
  • your Ask Odessa conversations
  • your saved items and Freedom File entries

Screenshots you share with Ask Odessa are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate a response and are not stored on our servers or database. They are not used to train AI models and are retained only briefly under Anthropic's commercial policy.

Retention: deletions take effect immediately in the app. Our database provider (Supabase) may retain limited backups for a short period under its own policies, after which the data is purged. We may retain minimal records only where required by law.

Important: cancel your subscription separately

Deleting your account or data does not automatically cancel a subscription billed by Apple or Google. To stop billing, cancel in your store account first:

  • Android: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → The Daily Oracle
  • iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → The Daily Oracle
